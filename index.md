Sui, Xinxin is a PhD candidate in the Environmental and Water Resources Engineering program at UT Austin. She has been named a Future Investigator in NASA Earth and Space Science and Technology (FINESST) since 2022. Her PhD research is about analyzing the urban influences on rainfall using remote sensing data. 

Before joining UT Austin, Sui used to work as a research assistant at Peking University, China, and the National University of Singapore. Her Master's and Undergraduate degrees are from TU Delft, Netherlands, and Jilin University, China. Her research interests include urban climate, hydrology, remote sensing, and machine learning.
